Hurghada life Tours

Experience Super Safari and a thrilling quad-band ride across the desert from Hurghada. You will be taken to your hotel and head to the desert. Away from the hustle and bustle of the big city and marvel at the wonders of the desert. Within an hour you will reach a Bedouin village, where you have the opportunity to experience the culture of the population and their daily lives. You can also enjoy riding (camel, horse and donkey) through desert sand. Enjoy the scenic views and see the beautiful sunset over the desert. Spend your day with a delicious barbecue dinner and grilled food. Enjoy a 45-minute quad ride. Enjoy the 20 minutes of the spider car, BBQ dinner with delicious grills, camel ride, horse and donkey ride in the desert Explore the desert in a four-wheeled Bedouin village and explore traditional culture, Bedouin pharmacy, traditional oriental shows, belly dancing, jeep safari, The locals have smoked a traditional water pipe
